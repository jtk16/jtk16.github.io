N-Dimensional Minesweeper

Explore the classic minesweeper game in multiple dimensions! Navigate through hyperspace, from familiar 2D grids to mind-bending 4D and 5D spaces.

How to Play

  • First click is always safe - creates a flood-fill opening like Google
  • Left-click to reveal cells, right-click to flag mines
  • 3D Mode: Toggle between grid slices and interactive 3D cube view
  • Higher Dimensions: See all slices arranged in grids
  • Numbers count neighboring mines across ALL dimensions
  • Touch Support: Tap to reveal, long-press to flag on mobile
Keyboard Shortcuts: Press '3' to toggle 3D views, 'N' for new game, 'H' for hints. In 3D cube mode, use arrow keys or mouse drag to rotate. Hold Shift+R to auto-rotate. Changing dimensions automatically starts a new game.
