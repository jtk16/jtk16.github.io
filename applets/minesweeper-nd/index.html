N-Dimensional Minesweeper

Explore the classic minesweeper game in multiple dimensions! Navigate through hyperspace, from familiar 2D grids to mind-bending 4D and 5D spaces.

How to Play

  • Click to reveal cells
  • Right-click to flag suspected mines
  • Navigate through dimensions using sliders or arrow keys
  • Win by revealing all non-mine cells
  • Avoid clicking on mines!
Navigation Tips: Use ↑/↓ arrows for Z-dimension, Page Up/Down for W-dimension. Red dots indicate mines in other slices, green dots show revealed areas.
