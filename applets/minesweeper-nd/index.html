N-Dimensional Minesweeper

Explore the classic minesweeper game in multiple dimensions! Navigate through hyperspace, from familiar 2D grids to mind-bending 4D and 5D spaces.

How to Play

  • Click to reveal cells in any dimension
  • Right-click to flag suspected mines
  • View all slices simultaneously - no navigation needed!
  • Win by revealing all non-mine cells across all dimensions
  • Look for patterns - mine counts span across dimensional boundaries
Multi-dimensional Tips: In 3D+ modes, you'll see all slices at once. Red dots on cells indicate mines in parallel slices at the same X,Y position. Numbers count mines from ALL neighboring cells across dimensions - not just the visible slice!
