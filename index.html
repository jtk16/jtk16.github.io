About Me

As a Robotics Engineering student at UC Santa Cruz, I'm fascinated by the convergence of quantum computing and artificial intelligence in robotics. My work focuses on developing intelligent systems that can learn, adapt, and make decisions in complex environments.

I specialize in reinforcement learning algorithms for autonomous navigation, computer vision for object detection and tracking, and exploring how quantum computing can accelerate machine learning processes. My goal is to create robots that are not just automated, but truly intelligent.

When I'm not coding or building robots, I enjoy contributing to open-source projects, reading about the latest advances in quantum algorithms, and mentoring fellow students in robotics competitions.
