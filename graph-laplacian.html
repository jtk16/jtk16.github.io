Understanding the Visualization
The graph structure is determined by the eigenvalues of its Laplacian matrix. Node positions reflect the graph's spectral properties, while edge weights are derived from eigenvector components. The spectral gap (difference between first two eigenvalues) indicates how well-connected the graph is.
