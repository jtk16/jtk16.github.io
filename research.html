Conference Best Paper Award
ICRA 2025 May 2025

Quantum-Enhanced Reinforcement Learning for Multi-Robot Path Planning

Jack Kinney, Sarah Chen, Dr. Michael Rodriguez

We present a novel quantum-classical hybrid approach to multi-robot path planning that leverages quantum annealing for global optimization while using deep reinforcement learning for local navigation. Our method demonstrates a 3.7x speedup over classical approaches in environments with high dimensional configuration spaces.
